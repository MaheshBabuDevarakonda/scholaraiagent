=== REGION: UNITED KINGDOM (UK) ===
- Study Visa (Student Route): Needs admission to a licensed sponsor. IELTS overall score of 6.0 (no band below 5.5). Financial requirement: £1,334 per month for up to 9 months inside London (£12,006 total) or £1,023 outside London (£9,207 total). Funds must be held for 28 days. Academic GPA above 60% equivalent.
- Work Visa (Skilled Worker Route): Needs a job offer from an approved UK employer with a Certificate of Sponsorship. Minimum salary threshold applies (generally £38,700 per year, or lower for specific shortage occupations/new entrants). English level of IELTS 4.0+ or equivalent. Age must be 18+. Standard visa fees and healthcare surcharge apply.
- Tourist Visa (Standard Visitor): Valid for up to 6 months. Must show intention to leave the UK at the end of the visit, proof of sufficient funds to cover all travel/accommodation costs without working (approx. ₹3-5 Lakhs liquid), and strong ties to home country (job, family, property). No age limit. No English score required.
- PR Visa (Indefinite Leave to Remain - ILR): Requires 5 years of continuous lawful residence in the UK under an eligible work category (like Skilled Worker). Minimum salary requirements must be maintained. Must pass the Life in the UK test and prove English proficiency (IELTS 4.0+ equivalent, unless exempt). No active criminal record.

=== REGION: CANADA ===
- Study Visa (SDS & Non-SDS): SDS requires direct entry admissions, paying 1st year tuition, buying $20,635 CAD GIC, and scoring IELTS 6.0 in all bands (or PTE 60). Non-SDS allows lower scores (IELTS 5.5 in one band) but requires higher proof of funds and has a higher rejection rate. GPA above 60% equivalent is standard.
- Work Visa (LMIA-based & Open Work Permit): LMIA-based requires a positive Labour Market Impact Assessment from a Canadian employer. Needs relevant education/work experience (2+ years). English score of IELTS 5.0+ equivalent. Open Work Permits are available for spouses of skilled workers or PGWP students.
- Tourist Visa (Temporary Resident Visa): Must demonstrate temporary intent, ties to home country (employment letter, family ties), and sufficient liquid funds (approx. ₹4-6 Lakhs) to cover duration of stay. No English language tests are needed. Age is not restricted.
- PR Visa (Express Entry / PNP): Points-based selection system (CRS) factoring Age (best points: 20-29; points drop to 0 after age 45), Education (Master's or PhD provides maximum points), Work Experience (3+ years skilled experience), and English proficiency (CLB 9/IELTS 8.0 Listening, 7.0 others provides maximum points). Settlement funds required (approx. $14,000 CAD for a single applicant).

=== REGION: UNITED STATES (USA) ===
- Study Visa (F-1 Student): Requires an I-20 form from an approved SEVP institution. Must show liquid funds covering the first year's cost of attendance (tuition + living), averaging $35,000 to $45,000 USD (approx. ₹30-40 Lakhs). IELTS 6.5+ (TOEFL 80+) preferred. GPAs above 3.0/4.0 (approx. 70%) preferred. Must show intent to return home.
- Work Visa (H-1B Specialty Occupation): Requires a US job offer in a specialty occupation, bachelor's degree or equivalent, and employer petition. Subject to an annual lottery. Minimum prevailing wage applies. Valid for up to 6 years. English proficiency expected but no official test mandatory.
- Tourist Visa (B-2 Visitor): For tourism, family visits, or medical treatment. Must prove strong ties to home country (job, assets, family) to overcome Section 214(b) immigrant intent presumption. Must show liquid funds of at least $4,000-$6,000 USD (approx. ₹3-5 Lakhs) for travel duration.
- PR Visa (Employment-Based Green Card / EB-1, EB-2, EB-3): EB-2/EB-3 requires employer sponsorship and PERM labor certification. Age is not a points factor, but education (Master's/Bachelor's) and progressive work experience (5+ years for EB-2) are mandatory. Funds depend on sponsorship status.

=== REGION: AUSTRALIA ===
- Study Visa (Subclass 500): Requires Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE). IELTS overall score of 6.0 (no band below 5.5). Financial requirement: living costs of at least $29,710 AUD per year plus tuition. Funds must be from approved sponsors (parents, self) with verifiable source of income.
- Work Visa (Temporary Skill Shortage - Subclass 482): Requires nomination by an approved employer. Must have at least 2 years of relevant work experience. English score of IELTS 5.0+ overall (no band below 4.5). Mandatory skills assessment for certain occupations.
- Tourist Visa (Subclass 600): Visitor stream requires proof of genuine temporary entrant status (employment, assets, family in home country) and financial capacity to support the stay (approx. ₹3-5 Lakhs). No English test needed.
- PR Visa (Skilled Independent - Subclass 189/190): Points-based system. Minimum points to apply is 65. Points awarded for Age (25-32 gives max 30 points; drops to 0 at age 45+), English (Superior IELTS 8.0 in all bands gives max 20 points), Skilled Employment (3/5/8 years), and Education (Bachelor/Master/PhD). Must pass a skills assessment. Settlement funds are required.
